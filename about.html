Who we are

The Legal Base (TLB) LPO is the trading name of Omnifix Technology Solutions Private Limited, a boutique outsourcing service provider, headquartered in India with corporate offices in the United States and United Kingdom.

With you, at every step!

Established in 2020, TLB LPO is the brain child of veteran senior attorneys and outsourcing industry experts with a wealth of global experience coming together to provide world class services to law firms , attorneys and legal departments of corporates across US , UK , Europe and Canada.

Your extended legal outsourced services partner!

At TLB, our aim is to provide a seamless and invaluable extension to our client teams and we believe in delivering value and competitive advantage at every stage of the legal service process, ensuring superior long-term returns. We pride ourselves on being a trusted and long-standing partner to our clients, offering customized and bespoke solutions that help deliver tangible cost savings.
